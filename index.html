The Framework

The Six Durable Skills

Students are remarkably good at the game we designed for them: hand them a list of boxes, they check every one. College and the workforce offer no boxes — students are handed an outcome and expected to figure out the rest. These six clusters name what that shift actually requires. Tap a card to see what each looks like in a student.

These clusters are meant to be kind to everyone in the room. Students aren't broken — they mastered the game we gave them. This framework names what the next game asks for.
