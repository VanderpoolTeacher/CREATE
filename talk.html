Prepare them for college and the workforce — woven into what you already teach.

The shared language

Six durable skills

  • Decomposition
  • Work & time organization
  • Working with ambiguity
  • Initiative / self-starting
  • Communication & presentation
  • Digital & systems fluency
[Riff on each from real experience — the student who can't break a project into steps, can't find their own file, won't ask the question until 11pm, freezes on an ambiguous brief, has never stood up to explain their work, treats software as magic. Especially the workforce-bound middle of the room.]
